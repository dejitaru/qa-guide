description: not yet
---
A defect/bug report should include:
<ol>
<li>Defect ID</li>
<li>Title/Summary</li>
<li>Description</li>
<li>Steps to Reproduce</li>
<li>Expected Result</li>
<li>Actual Result</li>
<li>Severity</li>
<li>Priority</li>
<li>Environment</li>
<li>Attachments (screenshots, logs)</li>
<li>Status</li>
<li>Assigned To</li>
</ol>
